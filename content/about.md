---
title: About Weston Village Hall
---

Weston Village Hall is here for everyone — a place to connect, celebrate, and share.

From weddings and birthdays to community groups and classes, we're proud to offer a welcoming space where great events happen. Whether it's a children's party, a coffee morning, or a local club, the hall provides a warm and adaptable setting for all occasions.

The hall itself is full of charm and character from its Victorian school heritage, with an elegant vaulted timber ceiling and tall windows that create a light, airy space.
