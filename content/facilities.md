A flexible space that adapts to your needs, from lively parties to focused meetings.

## Main hall

- Capacity: up to 60 guests (55 seated)
- Dimensions: 5.3m x 10.25m
- Elegant vaulted timber ceiling and tall windows
- High ceiling and electric heating

## Outdoor spaces

- Attractive hard-standing courtyard, once the old school playground
- Furnished with picnic benches for relaxed outdoor use
- Small side garden with planters
- Direct access from the hall for seamless indoor–outdoor flow

## Kitchen

- Fully equipped kitchen for catering and refreshments

## Digital & technical facilities

- Wi-Fi (5G / 4G coverage)
- Music licence and PA system
- Projector and screen
- Hearing loop

## Hire rates

| | Rate |

| Non-residents | £10.00 / hour |

## Ideal for

1. **One-off events** — parties, celebrations, workshops, and exhibitions
2. **Regular classes** — art, gardening, yoga, or fitness sessions. We're happy to help new groups get started
3. **Business & training** — a peaceful setting for meetings, courses, or away days
4. **Community use** — a welcoming venue for clubs, social gatherings, and local projects
